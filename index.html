About

Hello there! My name is Eric Harrison Jr, and I am a UX/UI designer. I am passionate about helping people solve problems and finding creative solutions to challenging design problems. When I'm not at work, you can find me spending time with my family and my dog.

But that's not all. Outside of the design world, I am also a 2021 Olympian in Track and Field as a sprinter, representing PUMA on a professional level. I've always had a competitive drive and a passion for pushing myself to be the best I can be, and this drive carries over to my work as a designer.

Design and web development have been a big part of my life for as long as I can remember , and I find joy in improving my skills and learning new things every day. I believe that great design has the power to positively impact people's lives and make the world a better place. As a UX/UI designer, I am excited to be able to use my skills and passion to make a difference and create meaningful experiences for people. Let's work together to create something amazing!"
